GPT (Game Point)

Full PMR Calculation :
[PMR 1.0] 0 - 500 GPT : 100 = PMR 0.2
[PMR 2.0] 500 - 1500 GPT : 100 = PMR 0.1

[PMR 5.0] 5000 - 8000 GPT : 300 = PMR 0.1
